Introduction:

At Spinergy, every movement has a function. Our system is designed to transform your physical effort into usable energy through an integrated and efficient process, connecting performance directly with sustainability.

Key Topics:

•Mechanical Conversion: The pedaling motion generated by the rider is captured through integrated generators, transforming kinetic energy into electrical energy through a controlled and efficient process.

•Efficient Capture: The system is engineered to minimize energy loss during conversion, ensuring that the maximum amount of effort is preserved and redirected into usable power.

•Immediate Usage: A portion of the generated energy is used instantly within the studio, directly supporting lighting, systems, or other operational needs in real time.

•Energy Storage: Excess energy can be stored and managed through supporting systems, allowing it to be redistributed when demand requires it.

•System Integration: All bikes operate within a connected network that monitors and optimizes total energy production across sessions.

•Reduced Dependency: By generating its own energy, the studio decreases reliance on external power sources, strengthening both sustainability and operational efficiency.
